Grid: 210 columns × 90 rows. Transparent cells: 18,900.
Underlay: none

Click or drag with Brush to paint cells. Bucket fill replaces a contiguous region of matching cells. Line draws a straight grid line from press to release, with live preview. When line snapping is enabled, the start and end points snap to nearby already-painted cells, which lets a new line connect cleanly to an existing line. In Cycle mode, the clicked cell, filled region, or new line advances from transparent to white to black and back to transparent. Hold Shift while painting, filling, or drawing a line to erase to transparent. Shortcuts: 1 Cycle, 2 Transparent, 3 White, 4 Black, B Brush, F Fill, L Line, Ctrl/⌘+Z Undo, Ctrl/⌘+Y or Ctrl/⌘+Shift+Z Redo, Ctrl/⌘+S Export.
